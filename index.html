About OMJ

OMJ is a community journal for management research. We publish preprints immediately, display anonymous reviewer comments and public editor notes, and keep every revised manuscript as its own citable version. The final accepted article receives a DOI.

Scope

Management, organizations, entrepreneurship, strategy, and related methods. Empirical, theoretical, and methodological work are welcome.

How it works

  1. Submit: Authors upload a manuscript and structured abstract.
  2. Preprint: A public preprint page appears with a stable citation.
  3. Review: Anonymous reviews are posted; editors add public decisions.
  4. Revise: Each revision gets a new citation; all versions remain online.
  5. Accept: The accepted version is typeset and assigned a DOI.

Submit a manuscript

Before submission, prepare a structured abstract (claim · evidence · why it matters). Ensure anonymization for double-blind review.
